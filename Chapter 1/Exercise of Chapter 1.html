Today's Recipe: Chicken Fajita Wrap

This Chicken Fajita Wrap is a great choice if you wish to grab a high-protein savoury meal, containing about 40g of protein per serving!

Ingredients:

  • 1 Tortilla
  • 2 pcs Chicken Breast
  • 2 tsp Smoked Paprika Powder
  • 2 tsp Garlic Powder
  • 1/2 tsp of Salt
  • 1/2 tsp of Pepper
  • Cooking Spray
  • 1 Red Onion
  • 1 Bell Pepper of any colour
  • Cream Cheese



    • Instructions:

    1. Grab the two chicken breasts and cut them vertically until it looks like 4 pieces. Cut them into smaller strips and add 2 tsp of Smoked Paprika and Garlic Powder, 1/2 tsp of Salt and Pepper, and Cooking Spray. Mix until the color changes.

    2. While the chicken marinates, chop the red onion and bell pepper into strips and put them in a bowl.

    3. Spray the pan with cooking spray and cook the chicken in medium heat until it becomes golden brown, then remove the chicken from the pan.

    4. Add the vegetables to the pan and stir for around 2-4 minutes in medium-low heat and once it is finished, return the chicken to the pan and mix well. With that, the filling is prepared.

    5. In a seperate pan, toast a tortilla untl perfectly toasted then spread it with cream cheese. Then add filling to the tortilla and wrap it. If you wish, you can put it on a clean pan and toast the wrap until crispy. Serve and enjoy!
